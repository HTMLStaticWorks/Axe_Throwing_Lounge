Our Story

AXORA Lounge

Where competitive energy meets luxury nightlife entertainment.

The Evolution of the Throw

Founded in 2024, AXORA Axes was created by two friends who wanted to transform standard adventure recreation into a premium night out. We wanted to move past industrial warehouses and create a welcoming, beautiful space for group activities.

By pairing top-tier structural safety targets with cozy leather seating, glowing visual graphics, and a curated food & craft drink menu, we created Metropolis's ultimate social competitive hub.

Our Mission

"To provide groups with an active, energetic, and completely safe social environment where they can build bonds, celebrate milestones, and create lasting memories over target games and premium hospitality."

No Compromise

World-Class Safety Standards

Axe throwing is incredibly safe when structured correctly. We implement safety configurations that go beyond standard requirements.

Full Anti-Bounce Systems

Our target lane floors are laid with dense shock-absorbent composite rubber to reduce axe rebound risk to absolute zero.

1-on-1 Coaching Supervision

Our certified 'Axe-perts' brief every thrower and remain at adjacent lanes to guide throwing angles and safety habits.

Steel Cage Enclosures

Lanes are fully partitioned with heavy steel safety chain links to ensure throwers have dedicated, isolated target areas.
