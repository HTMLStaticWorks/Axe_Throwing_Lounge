Active Safety Protocol

Our number one priority is keeping you safe. Our lanes are fully enclosed by structural steel safety cages, and throwing guidelines are strictly supervised by accredited range coaches.

  • Closed-toe shoes required
  • One thrower per sub-lane area
  • Thorough safety briefing before every session
